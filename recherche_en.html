Research interests:

I am interested in surface groups representations into various Lie groups. More precisely, I study character varieties associated to various Lie groups, as well as higher Teichmüller spaces I am also interested in notions of positivity in flags varieties.

I also study spectral networks and their interactions with the notions mentions above.
